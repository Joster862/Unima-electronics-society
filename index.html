Who we are

The Electronics society under the physics and electronics department at the University of Malawi was founded in 2023. since its inception, the society has been a vibrant hub for students passionate about electronics, circuit desighn,and microcontroller programming

Our vision

To promote the hobby and practice of electronics while equipping students with practical electronics and microcontoller skills achieved through structured learning, mentorship,hands-on experimentation,and active participation.
